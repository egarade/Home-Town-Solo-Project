Visit to Nigeria

Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa. It has 36 states with the population of over 218million people.

Some activities and fun facts in Nigeria

Zuma Rock at the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja

Zuma rock is very huge tall magnificient rock. One of its common feature is that it has a human face on it.

Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport

Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport is an international airport serving Abuja, in FCT Nigeria. It is the main airport serving the Nigerian capital city and was named after Nigeria's first President, Nnamdi Azikiwe.

Owu Waterfall at Kwara State, Nigeria

It's the highest waterfall in West Africa measuring 120m above the water level and cascades 330 feet down an escarpment, with rocky out crops to a pool of ice-cold water below.
